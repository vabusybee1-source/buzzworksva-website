How Buzzworks VA Works

Get started in just 5 days with our simple, risk-free process

From Consultation to Working VA in 5 Days

No setup fees. No long contracts. No risk. Just results.

1

Discovery Call

30-minute Zoom consultation

What We'll Cover:

  • Your business goals and current challenges
  • Transaction volume and lead flow
  • Current systems (CRM, dialers, transaction management)
  • Which VA role best fits your needs
  • Expected results and ROI
Outcome: Clear understanding of which VA service will help you most
2

Proposal & Agreement

Same day turnaround

What You'll Receive:

  • Customized proposal with transparent pricing
  • Service agreement outlining expectations
  • Invoice for first 2 weeks only (e.g., $480 for full-time at $6/hour)
  • No setup fees or security deposit required yet
Outcome: Review at your pace, sign when ready, minimal upfront investment
3

Payment & VA Matching

24 hours after payment received

Behind the Scenes:

  • Payment processed and confirmed
  • We match you with a pre-vetted VA from our bench
  • VA selected based on your specific needs and industry
  • Review VA's background, experience, and skills
  • Confirm the match meets your requirements
Outcome: Perfect VA match identified and ready for introduction
4

Introduction & Setup

60-minute onboarding call

What Happens:

  • 3-way introduction call (you, us, your VA)
  • Grant VA access to systems (CRM, dialer, calendar)
  • Share scripts, call lists, and standard procedures
  • Set expectations and success metrics
  • Schedule daily check-ins for first week
Outcome: VA has everything needed to start working tomorrow
5

VA Starts Work

Your VA begins working

First Week:

  • VA begins with training and shadowing (if applicable)
  • Daily check-ins to address questions
  • Real-time adjustments to scripts and processes
  • Performance tracking and reporting begins
  • Open communication throughout adjustment period
Outcome: VA is productive from day one, with support to ensure success
